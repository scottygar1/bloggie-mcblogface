#2020month

25Aug

So a month has passed in the blink of an eye.

  • 2020 has commenced in service layer. With a bang.
    • I am the sole Tibco dev.
    • Full development of all services required.
    • Integration of services with new tools.
    • Tunnels within tunnels within tunnels.

The last 4 weeks has been a crazy blur. 2020 has taken over my life, caused me more political nightmares than I ever knew were possible and proved to me that I have a lot of skill with the tools I’m working with.

My service layer is almost complete, built from scratch with very little help - this is my first large scale display of what I have achieved over the last year. And I have the small plus of people referring to me as the lead Tibco dev on the project. That felt good.

The new tools are both great and horrific in the same breath. They do some awesome things but can be horrible to talk to and get information from. I did enjoy being able to go back and make requests of other people though, made me feel like I’m gaining credibility with the other devs.

Tunnelling is my new favourite past-time. Or at least you’d think it is. It’s been a huge challenge to get my head around where I need to tunnel to and how tunnels even work.

My record so far is 9 tunnels at once. It’s not something I’m proud of.

Learnings: Don’t be afraid to put yourself forward and ask the difficult questions. DP has proved that it can actually be very beneficial and people have listened to the request and given us a lot of information.
