It’s been 2 months. It’s been a long 2 months.

  • PQQ Service dev complete
  • 2020 stalled
  • Jenkinsfile is a pain in the arse
  • Gradle is worse
  • New nexus doesn’t work as planned

A long 2 months, but somewhat productive. I’ve been stretched a bit by getting involved with the PQQ service. WS Security proved to be a nightmare beyond belief, which was fixed by DM reverting to one of my earlier builds - Though I was informed this wasn’t good enough at the time..

2020 is still messy. Decisions are.. lacking or being overruled by a greater being, somewhere in the ether. I’ve decided to press on with my micro-services idea, though I think it’ll be shelved by DP. I don’t think new things make him tick.

All the while, I’ve been playing with Jenkins and trying to get the new version to play nice with Tibco. It hasn’t, yet.
So I thought I’d try pulling the build into gradle, rather than maven.

It still hasn’t.

New nexus upgrades have also taken place at the same time - which will be a great task of updating all the pointers to the new nexus at some stage. There are many.

Learnings: I have a better idea about how things work than I thought I did. However, the way it is done is messy and even messier to unpick. Don’t run old builds on a stable environment. It overwrites stuff.
